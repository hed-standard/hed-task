# Reasoning and Problem-Solving

**Scope:** Deductive, inductive, causal, analogical, mathematical reasoning; hypothesis testing; categorization; insight; means-ends analysis; planning; subgoaling.

**Out of scope:** "Reasoning" as umbrella; "logical reasoning" as umbrella over deductive + inductive; fluid intelligence as an individual-difference construct.

**Open issues:** None open.

(hed-means-ends-analysis)=

## Means-ends analysis

**Process ID:** `hed_means_ends_analysis`

Iterative problem-solving strategy in which the distance between the current state and the goal state is reduced by selecting operators that diminish the largest remaining difference.

### Tasks

The following tasks engage this process:

- [Tower of London Task](../tasks/hedtsk_tower_of_london.md)

### Fundamental references

- Newell A & Simon HA (1972) *Human Problem Solving* Prentice-Hall
- Atwood ME & Polson PG (1976) *Cognitive Psychology* 8:191-216

### Recent references

- Anderson JR (2005) *Cognitive Science* 29:313-341
- Huys QJM et al. (2015) *PLoS Computational Biology* 11:e1004112

## Subgoaling

**Process ID:** `hed_subgoaling`

Decomposition of a goal into an ordered sequence of subgoals whose satisfaction advances the overall task; dissociable from means-ends search and often studied through hierarchical task analysis.

### Tasks

The following tasks engage this process:

- [Tower of London Task](../tasks/hedtsk_tower_of_london.md)

### Fundamental references

### Recent references

- Balaguer J et al. (2016) *Neuron* 90:893-903
